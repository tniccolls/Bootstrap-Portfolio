About Me...

Hello! I'm Tyler Niccolls. I graduated from UC Davis with a bachelor's in Economics, but I took a few Computer Science courses along the way. Desiring a career change in 2018, I enrolled in a coding BootCamp to learn Web Design, and I'm now looking start a career in this field!

While attending UC Davis, I began an internship at DMG MORI. I worked in procurement, and I thoroughly enjoyed my internship there. I returned to a full time position with the company after graduating, but as the company changed significantly over the two years I worked there, I started looking for other opportunities.

Since I enjoyed the introductory computer science classes I took at UC Davis, I considered different career opportunities involving coding, and decided on taking the UC Davis Extention Web Design Bootcamp full time to quickly develop the skills needed to begin a career in this field. And now, I'm glad I did. I love the logical process of coding, and enjoy learning new tools and skills everyday to develop as a web designer. I find it satisfying to build useful, working applications, and hope to build a career doing so. For some examples of projects I've worked on and applications I've built, please visit my "Portfolio" page.
